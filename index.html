Our Foundation

The principles
that guide us

Every line of code, every system we build is rooted in these beliefs. This is the foundation of Africa's technological future.

01
Mission

To imagine, engineer, and scale systems that make Africa work better. Building tools that bridge creativity, code, and community.

02
Vision

A future where African-built technology powers education, commerce, and innovation. Systems designed for real people in real places.

03
Philosophy

We believe in measured innovation: technology that serves, not dazzles. Our work blends rigor with empathy, designing for impact, longevity, and cultural context.

What We Build

Products in motion

We build for the people, not the ideal user with fast internet and the latest phone. Every product is designed to work in data-scarce environments, because real impact means reaching everyone.

Education
🏫
Ubora Écoles

A digital learning platform built for African schools, designed to work on low bandwidth, limited data, and entry-level devices. When a student is struggling, parents receive smart recommendations and can connect directly to a certified tutor through Ezra Service with one tap. Built for the classroom as it actually exists.

Live · uboraecoles.com
Services
⚙️
Ezra Service

A services marketplace connecting skilled providers with clients across Africa. Lightweight, fast-loading, and built for users with limited data. Because access to opportunity shouldn't depend on a strong Wi-Fi signal.

Live · ezraservice.com
Real Estate & Lifestyle
🏡
Ubora Living

A modern housing platform built for Africa's urban generation. Tenants build a Rental Score based on on-time payments, giving landlords a trusted way to choose tenants. When something breaks, tenants alert repair professionals directly through the platform. If none are available, landlords can book from Ezra Service in seconds. Better relationships. Fewer disputes. Smarter living.

Live · uboraliving.com
Fintech Infrastructure
💳
Ubora Pay

A unified payment API hub connecting MTN Mobile Money, Orange Money, and more, all in one place. When operators update their APIs, only Ubora Pay updates and every product built on it stays working automatically. We are also opening this infrastructure to African entrepreneurs: integrate once, go live fast, and pay only a small percentage per transaction. No technical headache. Just build.

On the Horizon
Healthcare
🏥
Ubora Health

A digital health platform modernizing how Africans manage their medical lives. No more lost carnets de santé. Medical records follow the patient, not the clinic. Streamlined communication between doctors, pharmacies, and patients, all in one secure place.

In Planning · 2027
Identity Infrastructure
🪪
Ubora ID

A secure digital identity layer for Africa. Ubora ID powers KYC for Ubora Pay, ties Rental Scores to verified individuals in Ubora Living, anchors patient records in Ubora Health, and verifies service providers on Ezra. One identity. Every platform. Available as an API for any African business that needs it.
